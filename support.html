Help & Support

Find answers to common questions or get in touch.

How do I add the app to my home screen?

On iOS Safari, tap the Share button and select "Add to Home Screen". On Android Chrome, tap the menu and select "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App".

Does the app work offline?

Yes! VolleyRef works completely offline. All match data is stored locally and syncs automatically when you reconnect to the internet.

How do I export a scoresheet?

After completing a match, tap the Export button to generate a PDF scoresheet. You can then share or save the PDF.

Can I edit a match after it's finished?

You can make corrections to completed matches from your match history. Open the match and use the edit function.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your account settings in the app. You'll retain access until your current billing period ends.

Is my data backed up?

Yes, your match data is automatically backed up to our servers when you're online. You can access your matches from any device.
