Rules

NFHS Rules. Handled.

NFHS sub rules aren't the same as USAV or FIVB. Different limits, different procedures. The app tracks every sub and blocks the illegal ones before you record them.

The NFHS libero can serve. but only in one rotational position. Not two. Not wherever. One. The app knows which one and won't let you break it.

Number of timeouts per set. Duration. The rules are specific, and coaches will absolutely try to call one they don't have. The app tracks what's left and enforces the limits.

High School Is Its Own Game.

New refs learning the craft. JV matches where nobody's watching. Varsity matches where everyone is. The rules apply the same to all of them. The app enforces them the same way every time. whether you're a veteran or it's your first night with a whistle.

Your First Year? The App Has Your Back.

The NFHS rulebook is thick. Sub procedures, libero restrictions, timeout limits. you're supposed to know all of it before the first serve. You won't. Nobody does. But the app does. When you try to record something illegal, it tells you why. You learn the rules by reffing with them enforced.

JV Doesn't Mean JV Rules.
