StreamingVolleyball Scoreboard Overlay

Put a live, auto-updating score on your volleyball stream.

To add a volleyball scoreboard overlay to your stream, score the match in VolleyRef.App, open its live shareable score link, and add that link as a Browser Source in OBS, vMix, or Streamlabs. The on-screen score then updates live as you score, with no manual typing during the match.

How To

Getting the Score On Screen.

  1. Score in the app. run the match in VolleyRef.App so the score is live and accurate. rally scoring, rotations, substitutions and the libero all tracked.
  2. Open the live share link. the app gives you a shareable live-score page that updates in real time.
  3. Add a Browser Source. in OBS / vMix / Streamlabs, add a Browser Source and paste that link as the URL.
  4. Size and place it. position the score where you want it on your scene, like any other overlay layer.
  5. Score as normal. every point, set and rotation you record updates the overlay automatically. no typing into the streaming software.

This is the same approach the big multi-sport scoreboard tools use, applied to volleyball with correct scoring built in. a live volleyball scoreboard that doubles as your broadcast graphic. For multi-court events, score each court and show whichever match you are streaming.

In the App

Score It With VolleyRef.App.

  • VolleyRef.App produces a live, shareable score link you can drop straight into your streaming software as a browser source.
  • The score follows real volleyball rules. rally scoring to 25, set rotations, substitutions and libero tracking. so your broadcast graphic is always correct.
  • Works for a single court or a whole tournament day.
