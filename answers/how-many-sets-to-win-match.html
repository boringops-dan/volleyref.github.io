Volleyball Rules Q&A

How many sets does a team need to win a volleyball match?

In a best-of-5 match, the first team to win 3 sets wins. In a best-of-3 match, the first team to win 2 sets wins. Sets 1-4 are played to 25 points; the deciding set to 15.

Full Explanation

Per FIVB Rule 6.1, a volleyball match is played as the best of 5 sets; the first team to win 3 sets wins the match. Sets 1 through 4 are played to 25 points with a minimum 2-point lead (no cap under FIVB/USAV/NCAA; NFHS caps at 27 per NFHS Rule 5-3-1). The deciding 5th set is played to 15 points with a minimum 2-point lead (NFHS caps at 17). Some competitions (youth, club, or recreational) use a best-of-3 format: first to 2 sets wins, with a deciding set 3 to 15 points. The format used is determined by the governing body or tournament director.
