How It Works

Get started in minutes. Score your first match today.

01

Set Up Your Match

Enter team names, select match format (best of 3 or 5 sets), and add players to each roster. You can save team templates to reuse later.

02

Enter Starting Lineups

Use the visual court editor to position players. Drag and drop to set positions 1-6. The app validates your lineup automatically.

03

Score the Match

Tap to award points. The app handles rotations, tracks substitutions, manages timeouts, and records everything. When you're done, export the official scoresheet.
