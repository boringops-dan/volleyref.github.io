# Volleyball Substitution Rules

Substitutions allow teams to replace players during a set. Libero replacements are separate and unlimited.

## Substitution Limits Per Set

- **FIVB:** 6 substitutions per team per set (FIVB Rule 15.5.1).
- **USAV domestic:** 12 substitutions per team per set.
- **NFHS (high school):** 18 substitutions per team per set (NFHS Rule 10-3).
- **NCAA:** 15 substitutions per team per set (NCAA Rule 10).

## Substitution Rules

- A substitute may only enter in the position of the player they replace (FIVB Rule 15.4).
- The original player may re-enter once, only in the position of the substitute.
- Substitutions must be requested and occur during a dead ball.
- Exceptional substitution (injury): a player unable to continue may be replaced by any legal substitute, even if the normal substitution pair has been exhausted.

## Libero Replacements

Libero replacements (FIVB Rule 19.3.2) are not counted as substitutions. They are unlimited per set, occur in the replacement zone between the attack line and end line, and can only be made for back-row players.
