The Problem

One Mistake. Everyone Notices.

Coaches protest. Players argue. And the rulebook keeps getting thicker.

Missed Rotations

It slips by until the other coach catches it. Now you're replaying points, replaying the argument, and replaying it in your head on the drive home.

Illegal Substitutions

Six subs per set. Positional restrictions. Libero replacements. Exceptional subs. You're tracking all of it in your head while both benches are yelling at you.

Features

The Rules Are Handled. Focus on the Match.

Substitution limits. Rotations. Sanctions. Enforced automatically. You stop second-guessing and start officiating.

Rules Enforced Automatically

FIVB, USAV, and NFHS rules built in. Illegal actions are blocked before they happen. Rotation errors? Impossible. The app won't let you make a mistake.

Match Options (All the Variations)

Every league has its quirks. Choose the ruleset, match format, and options you need. VolleyRef supports the variations, and then enforces them during play.

Smart Substitution Tracking

Regular subs. Exceptional subs. Multiple subs at once. Every limit tracked per set. Try to record an illegal sub. The app says no.

Libero Tracking

Wait-a-rally enforcement. Positional restrictions. Re-entry rules. The app handles it so you do not have to count rallies in your head. Optional settings support libero front-court play, or automatic sub-off when the libero reaches the front row.

Libero “Teleportation”

Normally the libero has to wait one point before returning. In libero-serving leagues there is an exception when the libero rotates back in to serve. VolleyRef tracks the libero serving position and keeps it consistent.

Sanctions & Disqualifications

Warnings. Penalties. Expulsions. DQs. When a player is disqualified, the exceptional substitution rules kick in automatically. No rulebook lookup required.

Built for the Court

Big buttons. Clear layout. Designed for the moment when both coaches are staring at you. Fast, clean, and zero hunting through menus.

Full Undo & Redo

Wrong tap on a point, sub, timeout, or sanction? Undo instantly. No restarting sets. No panic.

Live Scoreboard Sharing

Share a live link. Cast to a TV, projector, or second screen. Parents in the stands and fans at home follow every point in real time.

How It Works

Setup. Score. Done.

Three steps from first whistle to finished scoresheet.

1

Set Up Your Match

Set up your teams and rosters, confirm captains and setters, then choose the ruleset and match format. You’re scoring before the captains finish the coin toss.

Match Options

Everything that matters before the first whistle, in one place.

  • Ruleset: USAV, NFHS, FIVB
  • Match format and scoring
  • Side of court and first serve
2

Enter Lineups

Drop players into position. From here, rotations, substitutions, and libero replacements are tracked automatically.

3

Score the Match

Tap to score. Every rotation, timeout, substitution, and sanction is recorded. Complete point-by-point history. Exportable scoresheet at the final whistle.
