The Problem

The Libero Rules Were Written to Confuse You.

Wait-a-Rally. In Your Head.

The libero just got replaced. One rally has to pass before they can re-enter. You're supposed to count that while scoring, tracking subs, and watching for rotation faults. Good luck.

Back Row Only. But Which Players?

The libero can only replace back-row players. Positions 5, 6, 1. After three rotations and two subs, you've lost track of who's where. The coach hasn't. And they'll tell you.

Libero Down. Now What?

The libero is injured. Re-designation rules kick in. Which ruleset allows it? What are the restrictions? FIVB, USAV, and NFHS all answer differently. Your rulebook is in your bag. Both coaches are staring at you.

Rules

Every Libero Rule. Enforced.

Libero leaves the court. One rally must pass before they come back. The app counts the rallies and blocks early re-entry. You don't touch it.

Positions 5, 6, 1. That's it. Try to put the libero in the front row and the app stops you. No argument. No mistake.

FIVB: the libero cannot serve. Period. USAV and NFHS: the libero can serve in one rotational position. The app knows which rule you're running and enforces it.

A libero swap is not a substitution. Different rules. Different tracking. The app keeps them separate so you never accidentally burn a sub on a libero replacement.

Libero injured? The app walks you through re-designation. FIVB, USAV, and NFHS all handle it differently. The app knows which one you're running.

How It Works

You Watch the Match. The App Watches the Libero.

Libero enters. Libero leaves. Every replacement is recorded, wait-a-rally is checked, positional restrictions are verified, and the scoresheet updates. You see exactly when the libero can come back in. No counting. No guessing.

You See Which Positions Are Open.

The app shows exactly where the libero can go based on the current rotation. Back row only. No guesswork. No mental rotation tracking while both benches are watching.

Three Rulesets. Three Sets of Libero Rules.

Can the libero serve? Depends on the ruleset. Re-designation after injury? Different answer for FIVB, USAV, and NFHS. The app knows which one you picked and applies the right rules. You just watch the match.
