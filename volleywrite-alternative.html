Why People Switch

The Three Reasons VolleyWrite Users Look for Alternatives.

iPad-Only is a Real Problem

VolleyWrite runs on iPad and Windows desktop. That's it. No Android, no iPhone, no web browser. If your tournament hands you an Android phone, or your school-issued device isn't an iPad, you're stuck. VolleyRef runs on every device with a browser.

$129.99/Year After the July 2024 Hike

VolleyWrite's iPad annual plan jumped to $129.99 in July 2024 -- a roughly 44% increase. And that's a subscription, not a lifetime purchase. Skip a year, lose access to all your historical scoresheets. VolleyRef is $50/year, with a $5/week option for tournament use.

No Spectator View, No Live Sharing

VolleyWrite has no way to share the live score with parents, coaches, or spectators. VolleyRef generates a shareable link that anyone can follow on their phone in real time -- no app install, no setup.

TL;DR

VolleyWrite is a deep officiating tool for iPad and Windows -- excellent rule enforcement, but expensive, platform-limited, and with no spectator sharing. VolleyRef.App offers the same rule enforcement (plus FIVB) on any device for less than half the price, with live scoreboard sharing and full undo. If you're not on iPad or Windows, VolleyWrite isn't an option at all.

What You Get Instead.

Any Device You Own

VolleyRef is a progressive web app. Open it in Chrome, Safari, or Firefox on any phone, tablet, or laptop. Android, iPhone, Windows, Mac, Chromebook -- it runs on all of them from the same URL. No app store, no download.

Four Rulesets Including FIVB

FIVB, USAV, NFHS, and NCAA rules are built in. VolleyWrite stops at USAV/NFHS/NCAA -- no FIVB. If you ref club or international events under FIVB rules, VolleyRef has you covered where VolleyWrite doesn't.

Undo Every Action Mid-Match

Tapped the wrong team's point? Accidentally called a timeout that wasn't taken? Undo it. Every action in VolleyRef -- score, substitution, timeout, libero swap -- has full undo and redo. Go back as many steps as you need.

Live Scoreboard -- One Link

Share a link. Parents in the stands open it on their phones. Coaches follow from the bench. Cast it to a TV or projector at the scorer's table. VolleyWrite has no equivalent -- its iOS Buddy app requires a separate device on the same WiFi network.

$50/Year -- or $5 for a Weekend

VolleyRef is $50/year for unlimited matches, or $5 for a single week. VolleyWrite's iPad annual is $129.99. There's no per-weekend option -- you pay the full season price or nothing. For tournament-only referees, VolleyRef's weekly plan is a fraction of the cost.

Works Offline

Score through a full match with no cell signal or WiFi. VolleyRef saves everything locally and syncs when you reconnect. Gym basements, outdoor courts, facilities with bad reception -- it doesn't matter.

To Be Fair

Where VolleyWrite Still Has an Edge.

We're not going to pretend VolleyWrite is bad. It's a mature product used at real NCAA championship events. Here's where it genuinely excels.

NCAA Tournament Track Record

VolleyWrite was used at the 2025 NCAA Men's Championship. If your institution has an existing relationship with VolleyWrite and requires it specifically, that may not be negotiable.

Predictive Substitution Prompts

VolleyWrite auto-suggests the next substitution based on lineup history, which can speed up workflow for scorekeepers who don't already know the rosters. VolleyRef tracks substitutions but doesn't auto-suggest the next one.

If your tournament software mandates VolleyWrite specifically, or you need sub-suggestion prompts for volunteer scorekeepers unfamiliar with the rosters, VolleyWrite may be the right call despite the limitations.
