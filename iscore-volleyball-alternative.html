What iScore Doesnt Do.

iScore is a capable stats tool for iOS. These are the gaps that send referees looking for something else.

No Android or Web Support

iScore requires an iPhone or iPad. If you have an Android phone, a Chromebook, or want to score on a laptop courtside, iScore isnt an option. VolleyRef.App works in any browser on any device, no install needed.

No Automatic Rule Enforcement

iScore records what you tell it. It wont stop you from making an illegal substitution, calling an extra timeout, or letting a libero violate their restrictions. The app doesnt know the rules -- you do all the work. VolleyRef.App enforces FIVB, USAV, NFHS, and NCAA rules automatically.

No Live Scoreboard Sharing

iScore shows the score on your device and nowhere else. Theres no shareable link for parents, no cast-to-TV feature for gym displays. VolleyRef.App generates a live scoreboard link with one tap that anyone can follow from their own device.

No Second Referee Support

iScore is a single-device app. If you run a match with a first and second referee on separate devices, iScore cant sync them. VolleyRef.App supports two-referee mode where both officials see the same match state in real time.

What You Get Instead.

VolleyRef is built for the referee workflow. Heres what that looks like in practice.

Any Device, Any Browser

Android, iOS, Windows, Mac, Chromebook. Open a browser tab and youre ready. Nothing to install or update. Tournament organizers especially love this -- no dependency on a specific phone brand.

Rules Enforced Automatically

Select your ruleset (FIVB, USAV, NFHS, or NCAA) and the app enforces it. Illegal substitutions are blocked before they happen. Timeout limits are tracked. Libero restrictions are monitored. You focus on the match, not the rulebook.

Live Scoreboard for Spectators

Tap share and get a link. Parents scan a QR code and follow along from the stands. You can also connect a TV or projector to show the live score courtside. No account needed for viewers.

Full Undo/Redo

Every action can be undone -- score changes, substitutions, timeouts, everything. If you tap the wrong team or make a mistake, undo it and keep going. Not just the last action. Complete history for the entire match.

PDF Scoresheet Export

Same as iScore -- a professional PDF scoresheet at the end of every match. Includes set scores, point-by-point logs, substitution records, and timeout history. Ready for the tournament director or your own files.

Two-Referee Mode

Invite a second official to join the same match on their own device. Both see the same state in real time. No more calling across the court to sync up. iScore has no equivalent.
