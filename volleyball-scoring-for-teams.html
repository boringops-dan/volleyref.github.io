The Problem

Nobody Wants the Clipboard.

Whose Turn Is It?

Every match, the same question. Nobody volunteers. The clipboard gets passed around like a hot potato. The scoresheet quality drops every time a new person touches it.

The Napkin Scoresheet

Match is over. The scoresheet looks like someone did long division during an earthquake. Scratched-out numbers. Mystery arrows. Good luck submitting that to the league.

Built for Teams

Your Team Deserves Better Than a Napkin.

Every Match. Saved.

Score a match. It's saved. Score another. Also saved. By mid-season, you've got a full archive. Pull up any match, any set, any lineup decision. Export the scoresheet for league submissions whenever you need it.

Anyone Can Score.

Coach. Parent. Team manager. The kid who lost the coin toss. Anyone can pick up the device and score. The app handles the rules. They just tap.

A Season of Receipts.

Twenty matches in and you've got real data. Which lineup works. When your subs made a difference. What happened in that set you lost by two. It's all there.
