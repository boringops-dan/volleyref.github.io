Common Questions

Good Questions

  • Do I need to install anything?

    No. Volley Ref is a web app. Open it in any browser on your phone, tablet, or laptop. You can add it to your home screen for one-tap access, but there's no app store download required.

  • Is it really free to start?

    Yes. Your first 3 matches are fully free with all features. No credit card required. After that, you can choose a weekly, monthly, or yearly plan that fits your schedule.

  • What rulesets are supported?

    FIVB, USA Volleyball (USAV), NFHS (high school), and NCAA. Each ruleset automatically enforces the correct substitution limits, timeout rules, and scoring format.

  • Can spectators follow along?

    Yes. The referee can share a QR code or link that lets anyone follow the live score and court layout on their own device. No account needed to watch.

  • What about scoresheets?

    When the match ends, generate a FIVB-format PDF scoresheet in one tap. Share it with league administrators, keep it for your records, or send it to the teams.
