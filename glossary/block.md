# Block - Volleyball Glossary

> A block is the action of players close to the net to intercept the ball coming from the opponent by reaching above the net. Per FIVB Rule 14.1.1, only front-row players may complete a block. A block contact does not count as one of the team's three permitted touches; after a block, the team still has three contacts available. Back-row players and the libero may not block or attempt to block.

**Ruleset:** FIVB
**Rule Reference:** FIVB Rule 14

**Related Terms:** back-row-attack, libero
