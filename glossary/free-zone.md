# Free Zone - Volleyball Glossary

> The free zone is the area outside the playing court boundaries within which players may move and complete actions. Per FIVB Rule 1.1.3, the free zone must be at least 3 meters wide on all sides of the court, with a minimum of 5 meters behind the end lines at the international level. The ball remains in play when it is played within the free zone (e.g., a dig near the sideline), provided it crosses back over the net legally.

**Ruleset:** FIVB
**Rule Reference:** FIVB Rule 1.1.3

**Related Terms:** service-zone
